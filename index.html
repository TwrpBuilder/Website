Hi, this is
TWRP Builder

Which means no more TWRPless devices!

TWRP Builder is a project aiming creating Team Win Recovery Project (TWRP) for the devices not having their own TWRP support yet. Thanks to this project, any device can get the support! Besides, creating request is not something hard. Just download the app, create an account, upload the neccessary files from your device and stay tuned! Once we get the TWRP recovery image ready for your device, you will be notified!
